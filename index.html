Research Interests

My research focuses on combining mathematical structure with machine learning to build computational models that are stable, interpretable, and capable of strong generalization. I work at the intersection of numerical methods, physically based simulation, and deep learning, developing data-assisted frameworks that let learning guide computation rather than replace it.
