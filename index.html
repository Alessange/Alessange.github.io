Towa Shixun Huang (黄诗迅)

I am a fourth-year undergraduate at University of Toronto studying Mathematics and Computer Science. During my undergraduate studies, I have been fortunate to be supervised by Professor Jude Kong and Professor Eitan Grinspun. I am part of the DGP Lab, where I work with Yue Chang.

I aim to combine mathematical structure with machine learning to build computational frameworks that are stable, interpretable, and capable of strong generalization. My work focuses on data-assisted methods grounded in optimization, differential equations, and numerical modeling.
